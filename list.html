Sana's Recipe for the Best Chicken Pizza

Ingredients

For the pizza dough:

  • 2 cups of bread flour
  • 1/2 tbsp dry yeast
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 200ml warm water

For the sauce:

  • 450gm tomato
  • 7 garlic cloves
  • basil leaves
  • salt to taste
  • 2tbsp olive oil
  • 1 big onion

For the toppings:

  • 200gm chicken
  • 5pcs mushrooms
  • 2pcs bell pepper(red,green or yellow)
  • 3tbsp olive oil
  • 1tsp chilli flakes
  • 1tsp black pepper powder
  • 1tsp white pepper powder
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • pizza seasoning
  • lime juice
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1tsp garam masala

Instructions

Dough

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients that are mentioned in the dough ingredients into the bread flour.
  2. Then, gradually add the warm water and knead the dough until it become soft.
  3. Then keep aside in air-tight container for 2hrs.

Sauce

  1. Boil the tomatoes.
  2. After boiling, blend the tomatoes.
  3. Finely chopped onion,garlic and basil.
  4. In a pan, heat oil.
  5. And, gradually add the chopped veges aspect basil(will add on last)
  6. Saute them on medium flame and add blended tomatoes.
  7. And, add the spices to the sauce.
  8. After cooking for 15mins add chopped basil.
  9. Cook for more 10mins until the basil left its taste into the sauce. After this turn off the gas.
  10. Then, keep aside to cool.
  11. Yay!! we successfully cooked world's best marinara.

Toppings

  1. Cut the chicken into strips.
  2. Add all the spices mentioned in above.
  3. Marinate it for 30-40 mins.
  4. Saute the chicken into medium flame for 15 min.
  5. Cut the bell paper into strips too.Or according to your preference.

Baking

  1. Preheat the oven for 400c for 15 mins
  2. In a tray put baking paper and with the help of roller pin shape the dough in circle
  3. Add the sauce and the toppings.
  4. Bake it form 15 min.
  5. After that cut them in 6 slice and serve hot to your family.
